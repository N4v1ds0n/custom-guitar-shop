About Otto Mann:

Otto Mann saw music in everything. As a child, he restored his father’s broken guitar, sparking a lifelong passion. By sixteen, he was crafting custom electric guitars in his parents’ garage, gaining a reputation for instruments with soul. He later founded Mann Custom Guitars, creating pieces played by world-renowned musicians. Yet, despite his success, Otto remained in his workshop, hands covered in sawdust, perfecting his craft. His story was one of passion, resilience, and the belief that music is something to be nurtured and shared.
